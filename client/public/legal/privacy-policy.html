Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 26, 2025

Last Updated: March 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how One Guy Productions, LLC (“we”, “us”, or “our”), the operator of the CVitaePRO platform (“CVitaePRO” or the “Service”), collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit or use our Service. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with United States laws and the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Username & password (hashed via bcrypt)
  • Resume content, including uploaded files and text input
  • Company/organization details for cover letter generation
  • Optional address fields (applicant and company)

b) Information We Automatically Collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser and device information
  • Timestamps and usage logs
  • API usage counts and rate limit flags

c) Cookies and Local Storage:

  • Maintain authentication sessions
  • Store autosave progress (resumes, form data)
  • Analyze general usage trends

2. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate resume/cover letter content via OpenAI API
  • To improve product functionality and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse
  • To comply with legal obligations under applicable laws

Legal Basis (GDPR Articles 6(1)(a), (b), and (f))

  • Consent (for optional fields)
  • Contractual necessity (to provide the Service)
  • Legitimate interest (product improvement, security)

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., OpenAI, for AI responses)
  • Hosting and database infrastructure providers
  • Legal authorities, when required by law or to defend legal claims

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you request deletion, we will securely remove your data from our systems, except where retention is legally required (e.g., audit logs).

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, including:

  • End-to-end encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Hashed passwords with bcrypt
  • Access control and monitoring
  • Rate limiting and abuse detection

Despite these efforts, no online system is completely secure. Use of CVitaePRO is at your own risk.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR (EU Users)

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access – Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification – Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure – Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction – Limit how your data is processed
  • Portability – Receive a copy in machine-readable format
  • Objection – Object to certain processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, contact:
privacy@cvitaepro.com

7. Your Rights Under U.S. Law (CCPA & Others)

If you are a California resident or subject to similar U.S. state laws, you have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of collected personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of any “sale” of personal data (we don’t sell data)
  • Designate an agent to submit a request on your behalf

You can make a verifiable consumer request by emailing us at:
privacy@cvitaepro.com

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data from anyone under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.

9. International Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and similar safeguards where required under GDPR.
