AI Fair Use Policy for CVitaePRO

Effective Date: March 26, 2025

This AI Fair Use Policy explains the permitted and prohibited uses of AI-generated content on CVitaePRO, a product of One Guy Productions, LLC. This policy is intended to ensure ethical, secure, and lawful use of artificial intelligence technologies integrated into our platform.

1. Permitted Uses

You may use CVitaePRO’s AI-generated content for:

  • Personal job-seeking efforts
  • Resume and cover letter drafting
  • Educational and career development
  • Inspiration for professional content creation

Users may edit, adapt, or supplement the generated content for their own legitimate and lawful purposes.

2. Prohibited Uses

You may not:

  • Submit generated resumes or content for academic credit or falsify credentials

Misuse of the platform may result in:

  • Account suspension

We may update this AI Fair Use Policy at any time. Material changes will be posted to the site with a new effective date.
