1. Purpose

This Agreement is to designate an EU-based Representative to act on behalf of One Guy Productions, LLC in relation to its data processing activities concerning individuals in the European Economic Area (EEA), in compliance with GDPR Article 27.

2. Duties of the Representative

  • Act as a contact point for supervisory authorities and data subjects in the EEA
  • Maintain a copy of the Company’s Record of Processing Activities (GDPR Article 30)
  • Cooperate with supervisory authorities in relation to any data processing inquiries
  • Forward any requests or inquiries to the Company in a timely manner

3. Exclusions

The Representative does not bear responsibility for the data processing activities of the Company. Liability for any GDPR violations remains solely with One Guy Productions, LLC.

4. Record-Keeping

The Representative agrees to:

  • Maintain and update a physical or electronic copy of the Company’s data processing documentation
  • Make this documentation available upon request by supervisory authorities

5. Term & Termination

This Agreement shall remain in effect for as long as the Company processes personal data of individuals in the EEA and does not have a physical presence within the EEA.
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days' written notice.
